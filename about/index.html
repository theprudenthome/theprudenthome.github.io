About

The Prudent Home publishes practical writing on household money and self-sufficient living: subscription audits, grocery comparisons, garden returns, appliance energy math, and the quiet substitutions that stack up over a year.

What we publish

  • Cost audits. We add up what a specific category really costs a typical household, and where money leaks.
  • DIY vs. subscribe. When is making it yourself cheaper than paying for it? We do the arithmetic.
  • Garden and kitchen returns. What a modest garden actually yields in dollars, and what preserving pays back.
  • Energy and utility math. Real power draws, real bills, real break-even points.

No hype pledge

  • We do not give investment, tax, or legal advice. Anything that touches personal finance is comparison and arithmetic, not guidance on markets.
  • Every number is labelled either as tracked (we recorded it) or approximate (researched; verify before acting on).
  • We name what did not work and where we were wrong.
  • No clickbait titles. No "make money fast". No affiliate recommendation for a product we have not used or priced against alternatives.

How posts are produced

Drafts are written with help from AI tools against a strict template that refuses to publish without cited figures and an honest limitations section. The editorial review, the numbers in every table, and the final sign-off are human.
