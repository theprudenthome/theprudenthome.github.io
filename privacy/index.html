Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What this site does with your data

Short version: this is a static website. We don't run user accounts, don't set identifying cookies, and don't collect personal information when you read an article. The sections below are the long version.

Cookies

The Prudent Home does not set any first-party cookies or use localStorage to identify individual readers. You don't need to dismiss a cookie banner because there is nothing to dismiss.

Analytics

We may use GoatCounter, a privacy-friendly analytics service, to count page views. GoatCounter does not use cookies, does not track individuals, and anonymises IP addresses. It collects aggregate statistics such as URL, referrer, browser family, country and device type. No personally identifying data is stored.

Affiliate and third-party links

Some posts on this site contain affiliate links (for example to Amazon). When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, the destination retailer may set its own cookies and may share a commission with us. The Prudent Home does not receive personally identifying information about these transactions; we only see aggregate sales reports from the affiliate network. See our disclosure page for details.

Links to other third-party sites (sources, reference material) may also set their own cookies when you visit them. Those sites have their own privacy policies over which we have no control.

Email and newsletter

If an email newsletter signup is offered and you choose to subscribe, we will store your email address solely to send you new-post notifications. You can unsubscribe at any time. We do not sell, rent, or share your email address.

Your rights

You can browse the site anonymously. There is no account to delete, no data we hold that you can request a copy of. If you have subscribed to a newsletter and wish to be forgotten, emailing us to unsubscribe is sufficient.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from them.

Changes

If we update this policy, we will change the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and describe the change here.

Contact

Questions about privacy on this site? See the contact page.
